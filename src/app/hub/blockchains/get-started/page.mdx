## Customizable oracle & pools

1. Azuro’s flexible oracle can be customized to any EVM-compatible blockchain.
2. You can choose yourself if you want to have a public or private liquidity pool.
3. The oracle can have custom settings for different chains. You choose which contracts to plug in based on your needs.
