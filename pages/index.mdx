# Azuro Protocol

<p className="mt-6">Welcome to Azuro's documentation!</p>
<p>Azuro is an infrastructure/liquidity layer for on-chain betting. It utilizes a new Liquidity Pool design, the Liquidity Tree, which creates and maintains market liquidity.</p>
<p>Azuro supports thousands of sports markets & other games, plus a rich feature set, which can be utilized by anyone permissionlessly for the creation of various apps, embedded integrations or derivative products.</p>

---

This documentation will be useful for those who want to build their own betting application or product. At the moment [Azuro](https://azuro.org) works on the Polygon and Gnosis chains.
