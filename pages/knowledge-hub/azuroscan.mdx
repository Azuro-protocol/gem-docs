# Azuroscan: Search & Discover

Azuroscan is our upcoming product release slated for late Q4-2024.

It will be Azuro’s front-facing onchain prediction markets explorer, allowing people to search and discover past and live events on the protocol.

We envision Azuroscan to be your gateway to world events, driven by Azuro prediction markets. Uncover insights, analyze trends, view historical odds, see betting flows, etc. — all in one place!
